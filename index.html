i.

Powder Brows & Hairstrokes

4 Days1:1 PrivateLive Model
€1,600

Two complete brow techniques in one course — powder shading and fine hairstroke work, combined into a soft dimensional result.

What you will learn
01
Skin Science & Anatomy
How pigment behaves across skin types, undertones and conditions — the foundation of lasting results.
02
Facial Mapping & Design
Golden ratio, goniometric measurement, brow architecture and symmetry for every face shape.
03
Colour Theory
Clinita colour system — pigment selection, mixing, warm/cool correction and post-healing colour evolution.
04
Machine Setup & Control
Dermograph configuration, needle selection, speed, depth and pressure for both techniques.
05
Powder Brow Technique
Shading gradient, density control, pixel precision — building a soft, defined powder result.
06
Hairstroke Technique
Fine hair-like strokes — understanding natural hair direction, stroke pattern and skin reading.
07
Hairstroke Needle Mastery
Liner needle control for hairstrokes — pressure, angle, speed and avoiding over-working the skin.
08
Hybrid Brow Approach
Combining powder shading with hairstrokes for a natural, dimensional and fully personalised result.
09
Healing & Aftercare
Clinita aftercare protocol, healing stages, client home-care and touch-up timing for both techniques.
10
Hygiene & Sterile Protocol
Full Clinita sterile working method — field preparation and cross-contamination prevention.
11
Client Consultation
Contraindications, informed consent, skin assessment and professional expectation setting.
12
Live Practice
Silicone skin exercises followed by a supervised treatment on a live model covering both techniques.

Lip Blush

3 Days1:1 PrivateLive Model
€1,300

Enhance, define and restore lips with a soft wash of colour — one of the fastest growing PMU services.

What you will learn
01
Lip Anatomy & Skin Science
Mucosal vs dry skin behaviour, pigment retention differences and the unique healing process of lip tissue.
02
Lip Design & Symmetry
Lip mapping, cupid's bow architecture, corrective reshaping and natural proportion for every mouth shape.
03
Colour Theory for Lips
Pigment selection for warm and cool tones, correcting discolouration and matching natural lip colour.
04
Machine Technique for Lips
Dermograph setup for delicate lip tissue — needle choice, working speed and pressure control.
05
Lip Blush Technique
Full procedure — outline, gradient fill, blush wash effect, feathered edge and building a luminous result.
06
Healing & Aftercare
Lip-specific healing stages, swelling management, colour loss during healing and touch-up protocol.
07
Contraindications & Consultation
Cold sore history, filler interactions, skin conditions, consent and managing client expectations.
08
Corrective Work Introduction
Basic principles of working over existing pigment, uneven colour and previous lip treatments.
09
Live Practice
Silicone skin exercises followed by a fully supervised lip blush treatment on a live model.

— Questions —

Before you book.

Do I need previous experience?

No — our basic courses are designed for beginners. If you already practise, we'll calibrate the curriculum to advance your technique under the Clinita protocol.

Will I work on a live model?

Yes — every course closes with a fully supervised live treatment. You bring one model for the practice day; we'll guide you on exactly what to arrange in the lead-up.

What happens after the course?

You receive your Clinita certificate, ongoing mentorship via WhatsApp, access to the MyClinita app, and an open invitation to bring case studies back into the studio.

Is a payment plan available?

Yes — flexible payment options are available. We discuss the arrangement privately, quietly, and tailored to your situation.

Do I keep the machine and kit?

Always. The certified Clinita rotary machine and full pigment kit are yours from day one and remain yours after graduation — no rental, no return.

How do I book a place?

Send a WhatsApp message or call us directly. We'll arrange a short conversation to understand your goals before confirming dates and the deposit.
